Civic Issue Portal

An Official Government Citizen Reporting System

About This Project

The Civic Issue Reporting System allows citizens to report and vote on local issues like potholes, streetlights, or garbage collection. It helps communities raise awareness and hold authorities accountable.

  • Report civic problems directly to government authorities
  • Vote and support issues raised by others in your locality
  • Track the status of reported issues
  • Access contact information for relevant ministries
  • Secure citizen login and registration
