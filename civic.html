About This Project

The Civic Issue Portal empowers citizens to report, track, and vote on local civic issues such as potholes, broken streetlights, sanitation, and more.

Features:

  • Report civic problems directly to authorities
  • Vote and support issues raised by others in your locality
  • Track the status of reported issues
  • Access contact information for relevant ministries
  • Secure citizen login and registration

This portal aims to increase transparency, accountability, and community engagement in civic management.
